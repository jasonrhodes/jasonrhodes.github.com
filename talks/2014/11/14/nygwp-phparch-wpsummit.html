Not Your Grandmother's WordPress

Abstract

You’re a responsible-yet-totally-hip developer staring at a brand new WordPress project—don’t panic! The WordPress team might support PHP 5.2 but you don’t have to. In fact, you can use a workflow that would make even a Laravel developer jealous. I’ll show you some set-up tricks, talk about how not to use version control, explain how to manage everything with Composer, and tell you why WP-CLI might be your best friend.
